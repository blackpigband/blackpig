About us:

Black pig play at pubs and clubs in the local area and they play indie rock. Black Pig do cover songs by artists like Guns N' Roses, Oasis, Radiohead & even more!

Our band has 2 great members who play guitar and sing. Blackpig sometimes includes special guests too!
